About

Partially Prescribed is an underground music collective and event project rooted in plugg, cloud rap, and ambient trap. Started from scratch because the shows we wanted to attend didn't exist yet.

We run small, intentional events in LA and Arizona. 40–50 people in a room that was never designed for anyone but you. No corporate backing. No algorithm deciding what makes the cut.

The beats come first. The flyers, the rooms, the scene — that's all secondary. But it matters too.
