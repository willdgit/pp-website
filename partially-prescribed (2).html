About

MUSIC + EVENTS.
UNDERGROUND ONLY.

Partially Prescribed is a beat production project and underground event collective between Los Angeles and Arizona. Small shows. Real rooms. Artists you know and ones to discover.

The name isn't an accident. The sound is hazy and floating — not trying to be, just honest. Plugg. Cloud rap. Ambient trap. The tag changes. The vibe doesn't.

If you found this page, you're probably already tapped in. If not — welcome to the network.
